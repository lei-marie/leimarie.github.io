About Me

My journey began seven months ago, when I decided to transition from healthcare. It started with reading some MDN Docs and researching what coding was and was hooked from there. What pulled me in was the fact that I knew so little about a field that produces so much of what we use today and for so many important things. It triggered the question, well what in my world do I see a need that I can use these new skills and develop something that will be useful. Keeping in mind where I come from is a tiny island in the middle of the South Pacific. Where technology is only really starting to take a foothold. And it's vital in this day and age to stay connected to the broader world.

Currently enrolled in MITs Professional Certificate Program, Full Stack Development with MERN. So far it has been an exciting journey. I have learned Javascript, HTML and CSS amongst other things. I utilize Git and GitHub for much of my projects. Simple as they may appear, it's quite the distance from where I started. Looking forward to developing more skill within each area.
